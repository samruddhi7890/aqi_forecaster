Understand the pollutants
💨
PM2.5 — Fine particles
The most dangerous pollutant. Tiny enough to enter your bloodstream.
🌫️
PM10 — Coarse dust
Larger particles from roads, construction, and wind-blown dust.
🚗
NO₂ — Nitrogen dioxide
Mainly from vehicle exhaust and power plants.
☀️
O₃ — Ground-level ozone
Forms in sunlight from vehicle fumes. Not the same as the protective ozone layer.
🔥
CO — Carbon monoxide
Invisible, odourless gas from incomplete combustion.
🏭
SO₂ — Sulphur dioxide
Released by burning coal and oil. Causes acid rain and breathing issues.
📊
What is AQI?
The Air Quality Index — a single number that summarises all pollutants.
